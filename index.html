Our Restaurant

Lorna Brissett-Romans is the franchise owner of 2 Golden Krust locations, one in 2540-B US HWY NO 22, Union, New Jersey and the other located at 467 Lyons Avenue in Newark, NJ. Lorna has over 20 years of experience in corporate events and the food industry.

Not only does Lorna have her 2 franchises, she also offers catering so that anyone can be able to enjoy excellent Caribbean foods at their events. Our mission is to provide our clients Quality Service; Commit to each project from beginning to end; and to ensure the Success of your event, big or small.
